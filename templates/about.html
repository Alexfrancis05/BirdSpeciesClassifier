About Us

Welcome to the Bird Species Classifier, a project dedicated to helping bird enthusiasts and researchers identify bird species using advanced machine learning techniques. Our mission is to make birdwatching more accessible and educational by providing an easy-to-use tool that leverages a deep learning model trained on a diverse dataset of bird images.

Developed by a team passionate about nature and technology, this application uses a convolutional neural network to classify images of birds with high accuracy. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, our classifier aims to enhance your experience in the wild.

Stay tuned for updates and new features as we continue to improve our platform!
